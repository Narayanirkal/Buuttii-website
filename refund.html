Overview

At Buuttii, we strive to deliver fresh, nutritious meals of the highest quality to schools and workplaces in Hubli. We understand that plans can change, and we want to ensure a transparent, fair policy regarding subscriptions, cancellations, and refunds.

Since our business relies on physical meal preparation and daily delivery coordinates, refunds are processed based on the status of meal preparation and scheduling cycles as detailed below.

Subscription Cancellations

Users can manage or cancel their recurring physical meal subscriptions directly through the Buuttii mobile application or by reaching out to our support team.

Cancellation requests must be submitted before the daily cutoff time for preparation (typically 24 hours prior to the scheduled delivery) to prevent the next meal from being prepared. Once preparation has commenced, the subscription payment for that specific delivery cycle becomes non-refundable.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are eligible under the following circumstances:

  • Service Failures: In the rare event that Buuttii fails to deliver a scheduled meal due to operational errors within our reasonable control (and not due to inaccurate address details provided by the user).
  • Quality Issues: Verified quality or safety issues reported immediately upon delivery with supporting evidence (e.g., photographs) and reviewed by our quality control team.
  • Pro-rata Cancellation: For prepaid monthly/weekly subscription plans cancelled early, refunds may be issued on a pro-rata basis for the remaining, unprepared daily meals, subject to a deduction of any promotional discounts availed during the initial order.

We reserve the right to refuse refund requests in cases of suspected fraud, policy abuse, or failure to provide accurate delivery details.

Duplicate Transactions and Payment Failures

In case of duplicate transactions or payment failures where the bank account/card is charged but the order is not confirmed on the platform:

The duplicate/failed transaction amount is typically auto-refunded by the respective payment gateway (PhonePe, Razorpay, etc.) or the issuing bank. If the amount is not credited back within 72 hours, users can contact us at contact@buuttii.com with payment proof.

Refund Processing Timelines

Approved refunds are initiated immediately and processed through our authorized payment gateway partners.

Once processed by Buuttii, the refund amount typically reflects in the original payment source (bank account, credit/debit card, or UPI wallet) within 5 to 7 business days, depending on the issuing bank or financial institution's processing timelines.
