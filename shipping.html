Overview

Buuttii is a meal delivery service, not a product shipping company. We prepare fresh meals daily and deliver them directly to schools, educational institutions, and corporate workplaces in the Hubli-Dharwad region of Karnataka through our own trained delivery personnel.

We do not ship packaged goods via courier or postal services. All deliveries are made in-person by our delivery team on fixed daily routes.

Delivery Areas

Meal deliveries are currently available only within the Hubli-Dharwad region of Karnataka, India. We serve:

  • School campuses and educational institutions
  • Corporate offices and workplace complexes
  • Other institutions within our active delivery routes

Users must confirm delivery coverage for their specific address within the Buuttii mobile application before activating a subscription plan.

Schedules & Timings

Meals are freshly prepared each morning and dispatched on fixed daily delivery routes. Delivery timing is aligned with school lunch hours and standard office break times.

Exact delivery time windows are displayed in the Buuttii app and are estimates. Slight variations may occur depending on route traffic or operational conditions.

Packaging & Delivery Method

All meals are delivered in high-grade, reusable stainless steel tiffin containers to ensure food stays warm, fresh, and hygienic — with no single-use plastic waste.

Our delivery personnel will collect your previous day's empty tiffin container and hand over a fresh, filled one at each delivery. Users are responsible for keeping the tiffin clean and ready for pickup at the time of delivery.

User Responsibility

To ensure smooth daily delivery, users must:

  • Provide accurate and complete delivery details — institution name, building/floor, classroom or department, and a reachable contact number.
  • Ensure that the recipient (or a designated person) is available at the delivery point during the scheduled delivery window.
  • Have the previous day's cleaned stainless steel tiffin ready for collection at the time of the next delivery.

Delivery failures caused by incorrect address details, unavailability of the recipient, or failure to return the tiffin do not qualify for a refund.

Order Cutoff Time

To place or modify an order for the next day's delivery, all requests must be submitted before the daily cutoff time as displayed in the Buuttii mobile application. Orders or modifications made after the cutoff time will be applied to the following delivery cycle.

Delays & Disruptions

Delivery may be delayed or temporarily suspended due to:

  • Extreme weather conditions or natural events
  • Traffic restrictions or road closures
  • Sudden school/office holidays or unexpected closures
  • Public strikes or force majeure events beyond our control

In such cases, affected subscribers will be notified as early as possible via the Buuttii app, WhatsApp, or SMS. Eligible credits or adjustments will be applied to your subscription in accordance with our Refund & Cancellation Policy.
