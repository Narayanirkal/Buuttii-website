Overview

Buuttii specializes in providing fresh, recurring physical meal subscription services delivered directly to schools, teachers, and working professionals in offices. Because our service delivers prepared physical meals daily, our shipping and delivery model follows a direct local fulfillment logistics network.

Delivery Areas

Daily meal deliveries are restricted to specific school campuses, educational institutions, and corporate office complexes within the Hubli-Dharwad region of Karnataka, India.

Users must confirm address coverage within the Buuttii mobile application prior to activating a weekly or monthly subscription plan.

Schedules & Timings

Meals are prepared fresh daily and dispatched directly on our logistics routes. Delivery is scheduled to coincide with typical school lunch hours and standard corporate recess times.

While we coordinate with institutional schedules, exact delivery timelines displayed on the platform are estimates and may slightly fluctuate depending on route conditions.

Packaging & Delivery Method

To maintain temperature, freshness, and environmental sustainability, Buuttii meals are delivered in high-grade, reusable stainless steel tiffin containers.

Our delivery personnel will swap your empty stainless steel tiffin container from the previous delivery day with a fresh, filled tiffin container on the current delivery day.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate institution names, building numbers, classrooms, department details, and contact numbers.
  • Ensuring the recipient is present or has designated a drop-off point (like the school security office or building reception) during delivery.
  • Returning the previous day's cleaned stainless steel tiffin container to our delivery representative at the time of delivery.

Any delivery failure or delay resulting from incorrect delivery information or unavailability of the recipient at the designated drop-off point does not qualify for a refund.

Delays & Disruptions

Delivery may be delayed or suspended in the event of extreme weather conditions, traffic restrictions, road closures, strikes, sudden school/office closures, or other force majeure events beyond Buuttii's reasonable control.

In such events, we will notify affected subscribers as early as possible via the mobile application, WhatsApp, or SMS regarding delivery rescheduling or adjustments.
