Account Deletion

Request Removal of Your Data

At Buuttii, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated information, you can submit a request by following the simple process below.

✉

Request Account Deletion

Send us an email and we'll take care of the rest.

contact@buuttii.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request • Include your registered mobile number

What Happens to Your Data

When your deletion request is processed, we will permanently remove the following data from our systems. Certain records may be retained as required by law.

🗑️
What Will Be Deleted
  • User profile information
  • Child profiles
  • Meal preferences
  • App account data
🔒
What May Be Retained
  • Payment and order records

Retained only if required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes.

Processing Timeline

Once we receive your request, our team will verify your identity and begin processing the deletion. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

⏱ Typically processed within 7 business days

How to Submit Your Request

Option 1: Instant In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can delete your account instantly from the Buuttii mobile application:

  1. Open the Buuttii App on your device
  2. Go to the Profile / Settings section
  3. Tap on Delete Account and confirm the prompt
Option 2: Deletion via Support Email

Alternatively, you can request manual account deletion from our support team:

  1. Compose an email to contact@buuttii.com
  2. Set the subject line to "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered mobile number in the email body

Have Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or our data practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy or contact us at contact@buuttii.com.
